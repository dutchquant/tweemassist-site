Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Overview

TweemAssist ("we", "us", "our") is a Chrome browser extension that enhances the experience of using the Tweem platform on X (Twitter). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what we do not do with it.

2. What We Collect

TweemAssist collects minimal, anonymous data:

  • Anonymous User ID — a randomly generated identifier stored locally in your browser. This is not linked to your name, email, X account, or any personal information.
  • Bet activity — when you click a Tweem button in the extension, we record the tweet URL, metric type (views/likes/retweets/replies), multiplier value, and your anonymous ID.
  • Active session pings — a heartbeat signal every 5 minutes while the extension is active, used only to count how many users are currently online.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your X (Twitter) username or account information
  • Your browsing history or any pages you visit outside of x.com
  • Any content you type or post on X
  • Payment information of any kind

4. How We Use the Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Display aggregated community stats (total bets, live users, top bet tweets) inside the extension and on our website
  • Improve the extension based on usage patterns

We do not sell, share, or trade your data with any third parties.

5. Data Storage

Collected data is stored securely in a Supabase database hosted in the European Union. Your anonymous ID is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted alongside any personal information.

6. Third-Party Services

TweemAssist uses industry-standard cloud infrastructure to operate its backend securely. No personal data is shared with any third-party service — only anonymous IDs and tweet URLs are transmitted.

7. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data linked to your identity, there is nothing to delete or export. If you wish to remove your anonymous usage data, you can clear your browser's localStorage at any time, which will generate a new anonymous ID.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of TweemAssist after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
